SECTION D

TERMS AND CONDITIONS (Read carefully)

1. You are not permitted to make digital recordings (audio or video) during class sessions with phones or other electronic means. You are only allowed to take notes with pen and paper.

2. The only academic materials required for this course are your pen, writing note, and Bible. Any other required material will be recommended or provided by the Principal. No other religious material or resource is allowed in class.

3. The class schedule must be strictly adhered to. Punctuality must be observed at all costs. Lateness to three classes will mean a forfeit of the Certificate.

4. No participant will be allowed to join the course after the first day. Registration closes after the first class session. Only fully registered participants will be admitted into the Course.

5. Absence in any one class will mean a forfeit of the Certificate. Absence in two or more class sessions will lead to expulsion from the Course.

6. All phones and mobile devices must be turned off or silenced during the class sessions. Calls and texts cannot be received, sent, or made during sessions.

7. Eating are drinking are not allowed in the hall during or off sessions. Food materials should also not be brought into the hall.

8. This is training for leaders and ministers of the Gospel. Hence, we must observe and exhibit modesty and responsibility in our appearance. Therefore, every male and female participant must follow a formal dress code.

9. Each participant must always bear their identity tags during sessions and around the premises for easy identification. We strongly suggest that the tags be borne to and from the venue to avoid any form of harassment or trouble.

10. All assignments and projects must be done appropriately and completed in stipulated time. We will not condone any form of malpractice or fraud in tasks and assignments given. We expect that the student of God’s Word will be responsible, faithful, and of honest report.

11. There are leaders in various capacities who are saddled with the responsibility of maintaining order and facilitating a fuller experience for you at this Bible Course. You are expected to cooperate with them and receive them in high honour and regard.

12. No form of unruliness or disorderliness, disregard or disobedience to instruction will be tolerated.

13. You will be assigned to Support Groups with other participants, and a Leader who will facilitate your participation in this Training for a richer experience. You will be given group assignments and projects, and you’re expected to work with your Group in finishing them.

14. You will be constantly observed and assessed during this training. We expect you to imbibe and exude a high sense of humility, character, and responsibility.

15. The LBTC leadership reserves the right to withhold your certificate at the end of the course if we observe any questionable character or reason for distrust in your conduct.

16. As part of the course outline, there will be an outreach to a rural community. Therefore, every participant must prepare adequately and be fully involved.

17. This Training Course is as spiritual as it is academic. The move of the Spirit will be mighty during and off the classes; all of these will add up to a rich experience of God’s Word and Power. Expect supernatural occurrences and activities and be prepared to participate in them.

I agree to abide by all the rules and regulations of the Course.
